About Jacqueline

Licensed Massage Therapist

MA61214900

Jacqueline (she/her) has felt called to massage her whole life. She wakes up every day excited to create space for people to heal, whether that's on the massage table, in an honest open conversation with a friend, or hosting events for people to express themselves fully.

Jacqueline's massage style is for those looking to slow down and come back into their body. She loves working with first time massage clients and opening people up to the incredible and diverse world of body work.

She takes special interest in working with clients recovering from trauma and substance use. She meets every client where they're at and treats each appointment as sacred.
